Description:

  DOM-Cubes of 5 is simply DM-Cubes of 5 with a single control point 
added. It's meant to be more of a 1 on 1 map than an 8 player fist 
fight.  I wouldn't recommend playing with more than 6 players.


Where to get this map   : http://members.xoom.com/BOBXXII/unreal.html

Installation
------------
   Unzip the file DOM-CubesOf5.unr into your UnrealTournament\maps directory.
You can then choose DOM-CubesOf5 from the "start practice session\deathmatch" menu.
