Installation
------------
Unzip the .unr file into your Unreal Tournament/maps/ directory. This will make it a domination level
if you wish also to play deathmatch, you must copy the file and rename the copy DM-TheWishingWell, then
paste both into the Unreal Tournament/maps/ directory.
