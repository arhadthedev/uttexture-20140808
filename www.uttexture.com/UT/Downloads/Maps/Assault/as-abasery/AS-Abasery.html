Objectives

Main objective is to destroy the enemy base. Why you ask?
You are a member of a large underground army called: 'The Dozens Army of Ten'. This organization has bases all over the world and has all kind of weapons available, but the US army has set up a small operation in order to locate all the bases and destroy them. The map where all the locations of the bases are printed on hangs in a bunker somewhere in the Rocky Mountains. You and your theme must breach the base and destroy the base and the map with it. Of course you can't do this without help from the organization. You must send the coordinates of the bunker to the artillery located somewhere in the north. After that a helicopter will come and pick you up. After that the artillery will finish the job.

Notes
Making a map on a P. 200 MMX is a hard job -After testing my map for the hundredth time, finding a little bug, fixing it in UnrealED 2.0, rebuilding it in 6 minutes- takes a lot of time. Spending more time on UnrealED than at homework lets me think of stopping with this waste of time... But once your map is completed you really look forward to the next one that will be bigger and better than the previous one!
And so shall it be....
