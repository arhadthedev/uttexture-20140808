Installation


Unzip the AS-Rebellion's *.unr file in your Unreal Tournament/Maps directory, then either doubleclick on it, or type 'open AS-Rebellion ' in the console or select the Map in a Botmatch or an Internet game.

*.unr files go in Maps/
*.utx files go in Textures/
*.umx files go in Music/
*.uax files go in Sounds/
*.u and *.int files go in System/


Extended Description


You and Your cellmates have had some rough time. Now You have decided to get out of the Penal Institute. Some guards have been bribed to let You have weapons and open all the cell doors simultaneously. You may start Your journey out of the prison.



The map should be very different from the existing assault maps. The defending of the first targets is very difficult if not impossible but the situation will turn around as the game progresses. The map also includes a bunch of monsters which are an essential part of the gameplay. To ensure their appearence on network, the map forces the bNoMosters=False.
