Installation
------------
Unzip the the .unr file and place it into the maps directories under your Unreal Tournament directory. Start a practice session, assault. Choose AS-SkaarjBase.unr from the list. Don't forget to check your mission objectives! :)

Author's Notes
--------------
Careful in the Docking Bay, the Bay doors CAN open!
Skaarj High Lords are notoriously ill tempered, don't get in his way, unless you're going to kill him. ;)
