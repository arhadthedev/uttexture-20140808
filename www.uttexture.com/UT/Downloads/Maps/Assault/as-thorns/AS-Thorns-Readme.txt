AS-Thorns

Original Author: [XA]PsychoP@t & [XA]Defiance

Thorns, brought to the AS arena.

To install simply copy the AS-Thorns.unr file in UT map directory.

Strategy tips
-------------
Defense:  Hold down the trigger, and jump up and down.

Offense:  Hold down the trigger, jump up and down, and run

I realize that the offensive team has an unfair additional level of complexity to deal with, however I was unable to "level the field".  My apologies.
