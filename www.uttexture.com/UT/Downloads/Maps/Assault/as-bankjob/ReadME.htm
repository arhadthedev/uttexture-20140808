AS - BANKJOB!
 
 
  • Game Story:
The Robbers have been locked in a Secure Warehouse by the Security Staff after an attempt to steal the Golden Trophy from the
Safe in the Federal Bank!, however they are planning to breakout & to retake the Golden Trophy which is worth an estimated:
$300.000, they are DETERMINED to WIN!
 
  • Robbers Objectives:
1. Destroy BOTH Security systems located in the WareHouse.
2. Enter the Federal Bank & locate the Safe.
3. The door leading to the Safe is locked out by the main Security system, the ONLY WAY!! is to find the main Fire Alarm,
activating this will override ALL security systems in the Bank!..
4. The Safe door is on a TimeLock, you must use explosives to gain access!!
5. ONLY once all the above has been accomplished can you take the prize!!, the GOLDEN TROPHY!!
 
Instructions:
 
Unzip AS-Bankjob.zip into a temp folder, move AS-Bankjob.unr to your x:\UnrealTournament\Maps folder.
all the .UTX files go in your x:\UnrealTournament\Textures folder..
 
Build Time:
 
Approx 3 Weeks on & off.
 
Known Bugs:
 
Sometimes the Bots can get confused about their objectives, I have tested this map for several hours & this doesnt happen too often!
If you do experience any problems, just restart the level & you will be fine!
