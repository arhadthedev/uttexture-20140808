--- Author's Notes

This is a small map made for 2 to 6 players (or bots).
I made this map 'cause I needed the practice. I also wanted something dark, cramped, and deadly, where I could cut heads off by bouncing razors around corners.

--- Play Information ---
Game                       : Unreal v.226
Level Name              : DMRicochet01
Bot Match                : Yes
Single Player           : No 
Cooperative             : No
Deathmatch             : Yes (2-6)
Dark Match              : No (it is plenty dark)
Known bugs            : None that effect the play. A few visuals. If you find any that effect the play, please let me know.
