---Installation---

Unzip "DM-Hibernia.unr" into the UnrealTournament/Maps folder.
Unzip "Neve.umx" into the UnrealTournament/Music folder.

--- Features ---

Go for a tour in the helicopter for a different twist on z-axis fighting (Hope your not scared of heights)!!!
Some frighteningly high jumps to the redeemer.(The prize is worth the risk)

  Plot :  
 
     Located 473 kilometers off the Canadian East Coast,
    the off-shore oilrig "Hibernia" was abandoned in 2024
    by the Exxor Petroleum Corporation.

      Seeing a ' golden ' entertainment oppurtunity Liandri Worldwide 
    purchased the derelict oil rig  to convert it into the 
    ultimate deathmatch arena complete with working cranes
    and an - OPERATING- Bell 875-LC helicopter.
