3-MAR-2000 =================================================================================

Using the map UT-Logo-Map.unr as a base I created this fiery backdrop for the Unreal Tournament
desktop.

To install, unzip the contents of the .zip into your UT maps folder and rename UT-logo-map.unr
to UT-logo-map.unr.bak and rename Fire-Logo-map.unr to UT-logo-map.unr

Now open your UnrealTournament.ini file located in your UT system folder and replace the line

LocalMap=cityintro.unr

with

LocalMap=UT-logo-map.unr

and done! To make the most of it deselect 'show desktop' under the options menu.
