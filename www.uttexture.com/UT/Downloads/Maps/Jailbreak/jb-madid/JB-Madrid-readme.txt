Extended description:
--------------------
The planet Madid is covered almost 90% with water. Deep down in the crust though are lots of
minerals. Two rival mining companies set up shop to start mining but problems with supplies made
them ditch thier effort. Now these two underwater bases have been convereted into a secret prison
facilty for Madrid's most notorious criminals.
