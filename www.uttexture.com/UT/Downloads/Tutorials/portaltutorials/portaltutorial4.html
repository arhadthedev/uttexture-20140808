UnrealEd Tutorial 4 - Adding a Monster and a Weapon by Cableless
 

This tutorial is a simple one. Adding items to an Unreal level is easy using the Browser.

Download the map for this Tutorial and install it in your C:\UNREAL\MAPS directory.

 

  
 

 

Adding a Monster
 

  1. In the Browse window select Classes.
  2. In the Browse window, click on the "-" next to Pawn to expand the list.
  3. In the Browse window, click on the "-" next to Scripted Pawns to expand the list.
  4. In the Browse window, click on the "-" next to Skaarj to expand the list.
  5. In the Browse window, click on the "-" next to Skaarj Warrior to expand the list.
  6. Select Skarrj Assasin.
  7. Right Click in where you want to place the Skaarj and select Add Skaarj Assassin Here. You should see an outline of the Skaarj appear in the Perspective window.
  8. CTRL - Right Click on the Skaarj Assassin and then using the red arrow as a reference, rotate the Skaarj in the direction you want it.
  9. Under the Options menu at the top of the screen select Rebuild.
  10. Save your level!

 

  
 

 

 

Adding a Weapon
 

  1. In the Browse window select Classes.
  2. In the Browse window, click on the "-" next to Inventory to expand the list.
  3. In the Browse window, click on the "-" next to Weapon to expand the list.
  4. Select Automag from the list.
  5. Right Click in where you want to place the Automag and select Add Automag Here. You should see an outline of the Automag appear in the Perspective window.
  6. Under the Options menu at the top of the screen select Rebuild.
  7. Save your level!
  8. Select Play Level under the File menu at the top of the screen.
