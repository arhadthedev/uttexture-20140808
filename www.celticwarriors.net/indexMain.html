UNREAL ANTHOLOGY / STEAM / GOG / VALVE USERS, PLEASE READ BELOW:


Anyone who wants to play online with UnrealGold from the Unreal Anthology or Steam or GOG or Valve will encounter a problem by playing online. Due to a file mismatch you will be unable to join the online servers.

Example version mismatch UnrealShare

 

or


Example version mismatch UnrealI

 

To fix this problem you need to download this Patch:

AnthologyUnrealGoldPatchV1.0.exe

The fix does replace two files:

UnrealI.u
Unrealshare.u

Helpfiles for UnrealGold

After downloading, run the fix, after that you should be able to join the online servers.
